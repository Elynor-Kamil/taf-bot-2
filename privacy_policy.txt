PRIVACY POLICY (TAF BOT)

WHAT END USER DATA DO WE STORE?
We do not store any personally identifiable data.
The TAF Discord bot is used only for immediate events and actions performed on the server. No data is stored as part of these processes.
In addition to not storing data, the TAF Discord bot is not enabled to share information between servers.

WHO DO WE SHARE DATA WITH?
We do not share any data with third parties.
